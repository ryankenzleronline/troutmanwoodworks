Meet Michael

A carpenter who treats your project like his own.

My name’s Michael Troutman, and I’m a custom carpenter and woodworker serving homeowners and businesses across Kentucky and Southern Indiana. From custom cabinets and kitchens to barn doors, bookcases, furniture and fine trim work, I bring your custom ideas to life.

If you’ve been searching for a custom carpenter near you, or a custom woodworking shop you can trust, I'd be honored to help. As a custom woodcrafter, I treat every piece of woodcraft as my own — built by hand, finished with care, and made to last for generations.
