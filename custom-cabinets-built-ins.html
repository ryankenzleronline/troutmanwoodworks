Area of expertise for Built-Ins & Cabinets

At Troutman Woodworks, I design and handcraft custom cabinets, built-in furniture, and floor-to-ceiling cabinetry for homes across Kentucky. If you’ve been looking for cabinet makers near you who actually build custom, every piece is made to measure for your space — no off-the-shelf boxes, no wasted inches.

Whether you need built-in entertainment centers, a built-in office, custom closets, or a full wall of cabinetry, the work is built on-site to fit perfectly and finished to last for generations. As both a cabinet builder and cabinet maker, I bring true woodworking cabinets — not flat-pack — to every room.
